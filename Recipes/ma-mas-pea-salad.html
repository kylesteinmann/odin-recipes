Ma-ma's Pea Salad

Description

No good BBQ is complete with out the best of sides. Pea salad is not the most well know dish, but it should be.

My mother has been making salad for every Thanksgiving and Christmas dinner. Since I can remember. It is my ablsolute favorite side. I think you will like it too.

Ingredients

  • Two Cans of peas (a bag of frozen if you want more of a snap)
  • 3 tablespoons of miracle whip or mayonaise
  • Half a block of velveeta
  • Half of a small onion
  • three hard boiled eggs

Steps

  1. Dice half an onion and add it to a mixing bowl
  2. Slice your velveeta and hard boiled eggs into quarter inch cubes and add to the mixing bowl
  3. Add your 3 tablespoons of mayonaise or miracle whip to the mixing bowel as lon as the peas
  4. Mix ingredients until thuroughly mixed and add more mayonaise or miracle whip until you get desired consistance.
  5. Let chill in the refriderator covered until ready to serve.
